Our Journey

2021

ArtSphere was founded with the vision to connect local artists and give them a platform to thrive.

2022

We launched our first collaboration tools, making it easier for artists to work together.

2023

The ArtSphere marketplace was introduced, enabling artists to sell their work directly.

2024

ArtSphere reached 10,000 artists and expanded into global markets, creating a worldwide creative community.
